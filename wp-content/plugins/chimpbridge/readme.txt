== Description ==

#### Send Emails to Your List in WordPress

Easily send to your MailChimp email list right from WordPress.  No need for you or your clients to learn how to use mailchimp.com or leave your WordPress site just to send an email.  Create and send an email just like you would create and publish a blog post.

[youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rb13tJ0_6fE]

#### ChimpBridge Features

- Connect with your MailChimp account in seconds

- Send emails to a MailChimp list just like creating a blog post
